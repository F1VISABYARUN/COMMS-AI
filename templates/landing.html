How Comms AI Operates

A seamless sequence that runs in the background while your team stays focused on talking to customers.

Step 01

Customer Calls In

A customer calls your Twilio number or schedules a callback. The system answers, bridges the call, and records the conversation.

Step 02

Gemini Processes Audio

Our advanced integration passes raw audio bytes to Gemini to perform speech transcription, intent tagging, and task list generation in one single step.

Step 03

Google Sheet Appended

The AI output automatically populates your spreadsheet. Your database syncs instantly, keeping your CRM records complete.

Step 04

Follow-up Dispatched

The background cron scheduler runs. If a callback is due, it triggers Twilio to send a follow-up WhatsApp or SMS template message immediately.
